Data Science

W3 Schools says data science is about

"data gathering, analysis and decision-making. Data Science is about finding patterns in data, through analysis, and making future predictions"1

Amazon states that both data science and AI are

"umbrella terms for methods and techniques related to understanding and using digital data"2

While IBM describes it as a combination of

"math and statistics, specialized programming, advanced analytics, artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning with specific subject matter expertise to uncover actionable insights hidden in an organization’s data"3

There are many different roles involved in data science4.

  1. Data Engineer: designs and implements big data infrastructure.
  2. Data Analyst: collects and interprets data.
  3. Data Scientist: a data analyst that also uses machine and deep learning methodologies.
  4. Data Architect: designs and implements systems that manage an organsiation's data.
  5. Machine Learning Engineer: a combination of sofware engineering and data science techniques and technologies in machine learning.
  6. Business Analyst: documents a business's products, services and data.
  7. Business Intelligence Analyst: similar to a business analyst but also has a reporting role.
  8. Data Manager: manages a team of data scientists.
